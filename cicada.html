Interesting facts about cicadas:

  • Adult cicadas only live about 4-6 weeks before they die. The majority of their lives are spent as nymphs underground.
  • The noises that they are infamous for are made by males. The females are silent.
  • The sound of cicadas can reach 120 decibels, which is about the level of a loud thunderclap.
  • Females lay 200-400 eggs.
  • Cicadas are edible and are actually eaten in many places.
